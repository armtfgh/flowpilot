REACTION NAME: Synthetic sulfur dioxide insertion sulfonylation. TARGET: sulfinate salt SO1. SCALE: 3.0 mmol. Stream A: aryl lithium equivalent in THF, 0.20 M, oxygen/moisture-sensitive, -20 °C. Gas reagent: sulfur dioxide SO2 gas introduced by MFC after Stream A is cooled. Batch reference: SO2 balloon at -20 °C for 90 min, then methyl iodide trapping offline, 70% yield. SO2 is toxic, mass-transfer limited, and the first contact is exothermic. Flow translation: one cold gas-liquid SO2 insertion coil with BPR and small holdup; methyl iodide trapping is offline only unless represented as a quench mixer, not a second reactor.
