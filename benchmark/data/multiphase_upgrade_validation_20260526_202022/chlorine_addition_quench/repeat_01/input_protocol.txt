REACTION NAME: Synthetic chlorination of activated alkene with chlorine gas. TARGET: vicinal dichloride CL1. SCALE: 2.0 mmol. Stream A: activated alkene in dry DCM, 0.20 M. Gas reagent: chlorine gas Cl2 introduced only through an MFC into a cold gas-liquid coil. Batch reference: -20 °C, Cl2 balloon, 20 min, 84% yield. Chlorine is toxic and exothermic; gas-liquid mass transfer and heat removal are critical. Flow translation: one cold gas-liquid chlorination reactor with small holdup, BPR or controlled gas outlet, downstream Na2S2O3 aqueous quench as inline mixer only, off-gas scrubber after outlet. Do not create a second reaction reactor for the quench.
